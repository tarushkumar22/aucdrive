Auc Drive

  • Offline Storage, Online Access – Accessible via local Wi-Fi and remote secure access.

  • ✔️ Store, access & share files from anywhere
  • ✔️ No subscriptions – one-time purchase only
  • ✔️ Supports SSD, SD card, and HDD storage
